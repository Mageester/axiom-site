**BACKGROUND:**
A. The Partners wish to associate themselves as partners in business.
B. This Agreement sets out the terms and conditions that govern the Partners within the Partnership.

**IN CONSIDERATION OF** and as a condition of the Partners entering into this Agreement and other valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which is hereby acknowledged, the Partners agree as follows:

### 1. FORMATION & NAME
By this Agreement the Partners enter into a general partnership (the "Partnership") in accordance with the laws of the Province of Ontario. The Partnership will operate under the name: **Axiom Infrastructure**.

### 2. OWNERSHIP & CAPITAL CONTRIBUTIONS
- **2.1 Equity Split:** The Partners will each have a 50% ownership interest in the Partnership.
- **2.2 Profit & Loss:** All profits, losses, and distributions shall be split evenly (50/50) between the Partners.
- **2.3 Initial Contribution:** Both Partners agree to contribute the initial sweat equity, technical engineering, and business development required to scale the operations.

### 3. ROLES & DUTIES
- Both Partners will devote substantially all their business time and effort to the growth and operation of the Partnership.
- The First Partner will primarily manage [Insert Specific Duties].
- The Second Partner will primarily manage [Insert Specific Duties].
- All critical business decisions must be made by unanimous mutual consent.

### 4. FINANCIAL AUTHORIZATION
- **4.1 Operating Expenses:** Any individual Partnership expense exceeding $2,000.00 CAD requires the explicit written or recorded consent of both Partners prior to authorization.
- **4.2 Signing Authority:** Contracts, agreements, and banking execution binding the Partnership over the sum of $5,000.00 CAD require dual signatures or unanimous documented approval.

### 5. VESTING & "WALK-AWAY" CLAUSE
- **5.1 12-Month Vesting:** The 50% equity stakes are subject to a 12-month cliff/vesting schedule from the date of this Agreement.
- **5.2 Walk-Away Provision:** If a Partner elects to leave the Partnership (a "Walk-Away") or ceases to contribute meaningful effort prior to the conclusion of the 12-month period, they will forfeit their right to equity ownership and future distributions. They will be entitled only to their mathematically accrued share of liquid cash generated up to the date of departure, minus any unfulfilled liabilities.
- **5.3 Post-Vesting:** After 12 months, equity is fully vested. If a Partner departs after this period, a formal buyout protocol using an independent valuation metric (e.g., 2x SDE) will be triggered.

### 6. CONFIDENTIALITY & NON-COMPETE
- The Partners agree to keep all trade secrets, client lists, and technical architectures confidential during and after the lifetime of the Partnership.
- No Partner may operate a competing web infrastructure agency serving the specific contractor verticals targeted by the Partnership while active, nor for 12 months following a departure.
