# AXIOM INFRASTRUCTURE
## CLIENT SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Agreement is made effective as of the signed date of the initial deposit.

**BETWEEN:**
**Axiom Infrastructure** ("the Agency")
AND
**[Client Legal Name]** ("the Client")

### 1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
The Agency will provide web infrastructure, design, development, and/or SEO services for the Client as detailed in the accepted Scope of Work / Proposal document. Any features, pages, or integrations not explicitly stated in the initial Scope of Work will be subject to subsequent phased billing and change orders.

### 2. COMPENSATION & MILESTONE SCHEDULE
- **2.1 Deposit:** A 50% non-refundable deposit of the total project fee is required to commence business engineering and system architecture.
- **2.2 Final Payment:** The remaining 50% balance is due immediately upon final staging deployment, prior to DNS crossover to the live public domain.
- **2.3 Edge Retainer:** If opted or required by the SOW, the $150/mo Infrastructure Retainer will be billed on the 1st of every month automatically via credit card following the public launch.

### 3. CLIENT CONTENT BOTTLENECK CLAUSE
- **3.1 Information Requirements:** The Client is responsible for providing necessary digital assets (logos, raw photography, specific legal copy, login credentials) required to complete the infrastructure.
- **3.2 Bottleneck Threshold:** If the project is stalled for more than **ten (10) consecutive business days** purely due to the Client failing to provide requisite content or feedback, the Agency reserves the right to:
   a) Deploy the site with premium placeholder assets, and 
   b) Invoice the final 50% milestone balance immediately.
- The Agency will inject the delayed client content at a later date under the monthly maintenance retainer or via an hourly update rate.

### 4. LATE PAYMENT & PAUSE CLAUSE
- All invoices are due Net-15 unless otherwise specified.
- **4.1 Service Pausing:** If any invoice (including the monthly retainer) remains unpaid 10 days past the due date, the Agency reserves the right to suspend all services. This includes pausing the active website hosted on the Agency's infrastructure and routing DNS to a localized suspension page until the balance is cleared.
- **4.2 Reactivation:** A $150 CAD reactivation fee will be applied to restore the repository and Edge network propagation following a suspension.

### 5. OWNERSHIP & INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
Upon final payment clearing, the Client assumes full ownership of the digital assets, front-end code, and brand copy produced. The Agency retains the right to use the project deliverables as part of its public portfolio and case studies. The underlying proprietary scraping/engine systems and backend workflows remain the intellectual property of the Agency.

### 6. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
The Agency relies on third-party SaaS networks (Cloudflare, Render, Google Workspace). The Agency cannot be held financially liable for third-party network outages, sudden API deprecations, or search engine ranking volatility. In no event shall the Agency's liability exceed the total fee paid by the Client under this Agreement.
